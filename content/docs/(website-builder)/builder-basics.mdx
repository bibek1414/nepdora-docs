# Website Builder Basics

The Nepdora Website Builder ([nepdora.com/builder](https://nepdora.com/builder)) is an intuitive, real-time tool that allows you to design and customize your website without touching a single line of code.

---

## Interface Overview

The builder is divided into four main areas:

### 1. The Canvas (Center)
The canvas is your live workspace. You can:
-   **Edit Text**: Click any text element to open the inline editor.
-   **Swap Images**: Click on an image to upload a new one or choose from the library.
-   **Reorder Sections**: Drag sections up or down to change the layout.
-   **Add Sections**: Hover between existing sections and click the "+" button.

### 2. Sidebars (Left & Right)
-   **Left Sidebar**: Manage your site's structure. You can add new pages, delete existing ones, or view the component outline of your current page.
-   **Right Sidebar (Settings)**: When you click on a section or element, the right sidebar opens to show specific customization options like colors, fonts, spacing, and logic settings.

### 3. Top Navigation
-   **Device Toggle**: Switch between Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile views to ensure your site is responsive.
-   **Undo/Redo**: Easily revert or re-apply changes.
-   **Preview**: See your site exactly as it will appear to visitors.
-   **Publish**: Deploy your changes to the live site.

---

## Automatic Page Generation

Nepdora is smart. When you add certain complex components, the builder automatically generates the necessary support pages for you:

-   **Products Section**: Automatically creates **Product Details**, **Checkout**, and **Order Confirmation** pages.
-   **Blog Section**: Automatically creates **Blog Details** pages.
-   **Services Section**: Automatically creates **Service Details** pages.

---

## SEO & Metadata

To ensure your site ranks well on search engines, you can configure SEO settings for every page:

1.  Click the **SEO Settings** icon in the sidebar or top bar.
2.  Provide a **Meta Title** and **Meta Description**.
3.  Customize the **URL Slug** (e.g., `/about-us`).
4.  Save your changes. These settings will be applied once you **Publish**.

---

## Real-time Collaboration

The builder uses advanced socket technology to ensure your changes are saved instantly. If you're working with a team, you'll see updates from other collaborators in real-time.
