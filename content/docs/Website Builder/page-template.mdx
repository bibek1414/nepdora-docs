# Choosing a Page Template

When building your website with **Nepdora**, you can start by selecting a predefined **page template**.  
Templates help you quickly set up a layout and design structure suited for your websiteâ€™s purpose.


---

## How to Choose a Template

1. Go to the **Page Templates** section in the sidebar.
2. Click on **Add New Page**.
3. From the **Template Selection Menu**, choose one.
4. Click on the page you want to create.

Once selected, Nepdora automatically loads the base layout for that template, allowing you to:
- Edit content directly.
- Customize design and components.
- Preview your changes live.
