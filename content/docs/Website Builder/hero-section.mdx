# Adding a Hero Section

The **Hero Section** is the first thing visitors see on your website.  
It usually includes a **background image**, **headline**, **subtext**, and a **call-to-action button**.  
In Nepdora, you can fully customize the hero section to match your website’s style.

---

## Hero Section Features

- 🖼 **Background Image** – Choose an image that fits your website theme.
- ✍️ **Editable Text** – Customize headlines, subheadings, and button text.
- 🎨 **Colors** – Change text, button, and background colors.
- 🔠 **Fonts & Sizes** – Adjust font style, size, and weight for all text.
- ⚡ **CTA Buttons** – Add one or more call-to-action buttons.

---

## Hero Section Templates

Nepdora offers several pre-built hero templates:

- **Image Hero** – Large background image with text overlay
- **Split Hero** – Image on one side, text on the other
- **Video Hero** – Optional video background for dynamic look

---

## How to Add a Hero Section

1. Go to the **Components** section in the sidebar.
2. Click **Add Component** → **Hero Section**.
3. Select a **Hero Template**:
   - `Image Hero`
   - `Split Hero`
   - `Video Hero`
4. Customize the content:
   - Upload or select a background image
   - Edit headline, subtext, and button text
5. Customize styles:
   - Change text colors, background overlays, and button colors
   - Adjust font sizes, styles, and spacing
6. Click **Save** to apply the hero section to your page.
