# Getting Started with Nepdora

Welcome to **Nepdora**, the ultimate platform for building and managing your online presence. Whether you're running an e-commerce store or a service-based business, Nepdora provides the tools you need to succeed.

---

## 1. Create Your Account

To begin your journey with Nepdora:

1.  Visit [nepdora.com/admin/signup](https://nepdora.com/admin/signup) and click **Sign Up**.
2.  Choose your preferred sign-up method (Google or Email/Password).
3.  Once verified, you'll be redirected to your **Main Dashboard**.

---

## 2. Onboarding: Setting Up Your Business

After signing up, you'll go through a quick onboarding process:

1.  **Business Information**: Provide your business name and details.
2.  **Website Type**: Choose between **Ecommerce** (if you're selling products) or **Service** (if you're offering services like consulting, repairs, etc.).
    - *Note: This selection will filter the templates and features available to you.*
3.  **Subdomain**: Choose a unique subdomain for your site (e.g., `yourbrand.nepdora.com`).

---

## 3. Selecting a Template

Once your business profile is set up, you'll be prompted to choose a template:

1.  Browse through the curated list of templates tailored to your business type.
2.  Use the **Preview** button to see how each template looks.
3.  Click **Import** to start building your site with that template.

> See the [Template Selection](./template-selection) guide for more details.

---

## 4. Using the Website Builder

The Nepdora Website Builder is a powerful drag-and-drop tool that allows you to customize every aspect of your site.

- **Add Sections**: Click the "+" buttons between sections to add new components like Heros, About Us, or Testimonials.
- **Edit Content**: Click directly on text or images to edit them.
- **Manage Pages**: Use the sidebar to add new pages or switch between existing ones.
- **SEO & Publishing**: Configure your page's SEO settings and click **Publish** to make your changes live.

---

## 5. Managing Your Business

Beyond the builder, your admin dashboard allows you to manage the day-to-day operations of your business:

- **Orders**: Track and fulfill customer purchases (for Ecommerce).
- **Products/Services**: Manage your inventory or service offerings.
- **Customers**: View and manage your customer database.
- **Analytics**: Track your site's performance.
