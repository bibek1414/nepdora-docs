# Order Management

The **Orders** section of your admin dashboard ([nepdora.com/admin/orders](https://nepdora.com/admin/orders)) is the central hub for managing your business's transactions. Whether your customers buy online or you create orders manually, you can track everything here.

---

## The Order List

The main order list provides a high-level view of all your transactions:

-   **Order Number**: A unique identifier for every purchase.
-   **Customer**: The name of the customer who placed the order.
-   **Payment Status**: Quickly see if an order is **Paid** or **Unpaid**.
-   **Total**: The total amount of the transaction.
-   **Status**: The current fulfillment status (e.g., Pending, Shipped, Delivered).

---

## Managing Order Status

Keeping your customers updated is crucial. You can update the status of any order directly from the list or the order details view:

1.  Locate the order in the list.
2.  Use the status dropdown to select a new state:
    -   **Pending**: Newly created orders.
    -   **Confirmed**: Order has been reviewed and accepted.
    -   **Processing**: Items are being prepared or packed.
    -   **Shipped**: Order is on its way to the customer.
    -   **Delivered**: Customer has received the order.
    -   **Cancelled**: Order has been voided.

---

## Manual Orders

Sometimes you may need to create an order manually (e.g., for a phone order or a walk-in customer):

1.  Click the **Create Manual Order** button.
2.  Fill in the customer details and select the products/services.
3.  Set the payment type (COD, eSewa, Khalti, etc.).
4.  The order will appear in your main list marked as a "Manual" order.

---

## POS (Point of Sale) Integration

If you have a physical location, Nepdora's POS system integrates directly with your main order management:

-   **Real-time Sync**: Orders created in the POS system appear immediately in your admin dashboard.
-   **POS Badge**: Easily identify POS orders in your list via a dedicated badge.
-   **Unified Inventory**: Sales in the POS automatically update your stock levels across the entire platform.

---

## Order Details

Clicking on any order opens the **Order Details** view, where you can:

-   See the full list of items purchased.
-   Review customer contact information and shipping address.
-   View payment transaction details.
-   Print invoices or shipping labels.

---

## Filtering and Search

As your business grows, finding specific orders becomes easier with our powerful search and filter tools:

-   **Search**: Search by order number or customer name.
-   **Status Filters**: View only "Pending" or "Shipped" orders.
-   **Manual Filter**: Quickly isolate orders created manually by your team.
