# Template Selection

Choosing the right template is the first step toward a stunning website. Nepdora offers a wide variety of professionally designed templates ([nepdora.com/admin/template](https://nepdora.com/admin/template)) optimized for different industries and business models.

---

## Business-Type Filtering

When you first set up your account, Nepdora asks for your business type. This helps us show you the most relevant templates:

-   **Ecommerce**: Templates optimized for high-conversion product pages, cart flows, and checkout experiences.
-   **Service**: Templates designed for lead generation, service showcases, and booking appointments.

---

## Finding a Template

### Search and Filters
Use the search bar at the top of the template library to find specific styles or industries (e.g., "Minimalist", "Agency", "Restaurant").

### Live Preview
Before committing to a template, you can click **Preview** to see a live demo. This allows you to explore the navigation, layout, and responsiveness of the template on different devices.

---

## Importing a Template

Once you've found the perfect template:

1.  Click the **Import** button.
2.  Nepdora will begin setting up your workspace. A loading screen will keep you updated on the progress.
3.  Once complete, the **Website Builder** will open with your new template loaded and ready for customization.

> [!TIP]
> Don't worry about making a perfect choice immediately. You can always change your template later from the **Admin > Template** section, although importing a new template may overwrite some of your current customizations.

---

## Template Components

Each template comes pre-packaged with a set of default pages and sections:

-   **Home**: The landing page designed to capture attention.
-   **Product/Service Details**: Automated templates for individual offerings.
-   **Contact Us**: Pre-configured forms for customer inquiries.
-   **Legal Pages**: Basic templates for Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
