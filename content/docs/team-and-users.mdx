## 🧑‍💼 Team Management

The **Team & Users** section allows you to manage your organization's team members or contributors directly from the website builder.  
Each team member can have details such as their name, role, contact information, and bio.

### ✨ Key Features

- **Easy Member Management** — Add, edit, or remove team members quickly.  
- **Profile Customization** — Upload profile photos, add bios, and contact details.  
- **Role Assignment** — Define clear responsibilities and display roles on the site.  
- **Real-time Updates** — Every change reflects instantly in your website preview.  

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. Navigate to the **Team & Users** section in the sidebar.  
2. Click **“Add Team Member”** to create a new profile.  
3. Enter details such as **name, role, about, email, and Social Media Links(if available)**.  
4. Save your changes — the builder automatically syncs them to your live preview.
