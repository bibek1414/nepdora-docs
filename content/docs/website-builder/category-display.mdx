# 🗂️ Category Display

The **Category Display** component in the Nepdora Website Builder allows you to organize your products into easily browsable categories.  
This makes your store more structured and improves the customer shopping experience by allowing them to filter and explore product types efficiently.

---

## 🧱 Adding a Category Display Section

1. In the **builder interface**, open the **Components** panel from the left sidebar.
2. Select **Category Display**.
3. The component will be added to your page, allowing visitors to browse through categories visually.
4. Once added, a **Manage Categories** button appears within the component toolbar.

---

## ⚙️ Managing Categories

Click **Manage Categories** to open the **Category Management Page**.  
Here you can create, edit, and organize your categories, each with its own title, description, and image.

---

## 🆕 Creating a New Category

Click **Create New Category** to open the category creation form.  
You’ll see the following input fields:

### 1. **Category Name**

Enter the title of your category.  
Example: `Electronics`, `Men’s Fashion`, or `Home Decor`.

<Callout type="tip" title="Tip">
  Use clear, descriptive names so customers can quickly understand what’s inside
  the category.
</Callout>

---

### 2. **Category Description**

Add a short description to provide context about what this category contains.  
This description may appear below the category title or on the category’s dedicated page.

Example:

> Explore the latest gadgets and electronic accessories designed to simplify your daily life.

---

### 3. **Category Image**

Upload an image or icon representing the category.

- Click **“Choose File”** or drag and drop an image into the upload area.
- Maximum file size: **5 MB**
- Supported formats: **JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF**

<Callout type="info" title="Accessibility Tip">
  Add clear, relevant images that visually represent the category’s theme (e.g.,
  a laptop image for “Electronics”).
</Callout>

---

### 4. **Actions**

Once you’ve filled out the category details:

- **Save / Create** → Adds the new category to your store.
- **Cancel** → Returns to the previous screen without saving.

After creation, your category appears in the **Category Display** component on your page automatically.

---

## 🧩 Editing Existing Categories

To edit a category:

1. Click the **Manage Categories** button.
2. Select the category you want to update.
3. Edit its name, description, or image.
4. Save changes — updates will reflect instantly on the website.

---

## 🖼️ Display Options

The **Category Display** component supports multiple visual layouts.  
You can switch between **grid**, **list**, or **card** views depending on your page design.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
  Use high-quality category images with consistent aspect ratios for a more
  professional, cohesive look.
</Callout>

---

## 🧠 Best Practices

- Keep category names short (1–3 words).
- Use **relevant images** for visual clarity.
- Avoid creating too many small categories — group similar items together.
- Write **SEO-friendly descriptions** (50–150 characters).
- Always ensure each product is assigned to at least one category.

---

## 🪄 Example Workflow

1. Add a **Category Display** component to your homepage.
2. Click **Manage Categories** → **Create New Category**.
3. Enter:
   - **Name:** “Electronics”
   - **Description:** “Explore the latest tech and gadgets.”
   - **Image:** Upload a high-quality product photo.
4. Click **Save**.
5. Repeat the process to add more categories (e.g., _Clothing_, _Accessories_, _Home Decor_).
6. Return to the builder — your categories now appear instantly in the Category Display section.
